# Privacy Policy

**SurfDesk** (surfdesk.com.au)
Last updated: 16 June 2026
Operator: AIwers Group | Birtinya, Queensland, Australia

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use SurfDesk, we may collect:

- **Account data**: Name, email address when you sign up
- **Location data**: General location (suburb/region) to provide relevant surf forecasts — only if you grant permission
- **Usage data**: Features used, sessions logged (via PostHog analytics)
- **Payment information**: Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store card details.
- **Device and browser data**: IP address, browser type, operating system

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To deliver surf forecasts, tide data, and co-working spot recommendations
- To process payments and manage your subscription via Stripe
- To send account and service-related notifications via Brevo
- To personalise your surf session recommendations
- To improve the SurfDesk platform

## 3. Location Data

If you grant location permission, we use your general location to provide relevant surf break and co-working spot data. We do not share precise location data with third parties. Location permission can be revoked at any time via your browser settings.

## 4. Payment Processing

Payments are handled by Stripe. SurfDesk does not store credit card information. See Stripe's privacy policy at https://stripe.com/au/privacy

## 5. Cookies

We use cookies for authentication, session management, and analytics (PostHog). By using SurfDesk, you consent to essential cookies required for the service.

## 6. Third-Party Services

- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **PostHog**: Analytics (US Cloud)
- **Brevo**: Email delivery
- **Vercel**: Hosting
- **Supabase**: Database and backend
- **Surf forecast APIs**: Tide and wave data providers

## 7. Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account closure.
- Analytics data: Retained for 12 months
- Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Australian tax law

## 8. Your Rights (Australian Privacy Act 1988)

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact: privacy@surfdesk.com.au
